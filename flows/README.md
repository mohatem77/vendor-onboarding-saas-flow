
# Vendor Onboarding Workflow

## Stage 1 – Supplier Registration
The supplier enters:
- company name
- email
- country
- tax/VAT number

## Stage 2 – Document Submission
Required documents:
- trade license
- VAT certificate
- bank details

## Stage 3 – Validation
System checks:
- required fields completed
- documents uploaded
- format validation

## Stage 4 – Internal Review
Finance and compliance teams review:
- completeness
- accuracy
- onboarding eligibility

## Stage 5 – Approval Workflow
Approval routing based on:
- company rules
- thresholds
- risk level

## Stage 6 – ERP-Ready Vendor Creation
Approved vendor is ready to be created in ERP systems.

## Objective

To create a structured, auditable, and scalable vendor onboarding process for enterprise environments.
